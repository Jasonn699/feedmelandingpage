Frequently Asked Questions

Does FeedMe POS work without an internet connection?

+

Yes. FeedMe POS supports offline mode. You can continue taking orders and printing receipts even if the internet goes down. All data will sync automatically once the connection is restored.

Can I integrate GrabFood and Foodpanda orders?

+

Yes. FeedMe integrates with GrabFood and Foodpanda. Orders from these platforms are synced directly into the POS system — no manual key-in required.

Is FeedMe POS suitable for small businesses?

+

Absolutely. Whether you run a café, food stall, or full-service restaurant, FeedMe is designed to fit your business size. Use only the features you need and scale up when ready.

How does FeedMe help manage kitchen operations?

+

FeedMe includes a Kitchen Display System (KDS) that sends orders directly from the POS to the kitchen screen. This reduces miscommunication and improves prep speed.

Can it support multiple branches or outlets?

+

Yes. You can manage multiple outlets under one system — each with its own menus, reports, and staff roles — all from a centralized dashboard.

Does it come with inventory tracking?

+

Yes. FeedMe includes inventory and ingredient management to help you track usage, reduce waste, and receive low-stock alerts in real time.

Will I get access to sales reports and analytics?

+

Yes. FeedMe provides real-time dashboards and detailed reports on sales, item performance, staff efficiency, and more — accessible anytime, anywhere.

What kind of support is available after setup?

+

We provide full onboarding and training. Ongoing support is available via WhatsApp, phone, or email. You're never left on your own.

Is the system easy to learn for staff?

+

Yes. FeedMe POS is designed to be simple and intuitive. Most teams can learn to use it with minimal training.

How much does it cost?

+

Pricing varies based on your business size and required modules. Contact us today for a free demo and a custom quote tailored to your needs.
